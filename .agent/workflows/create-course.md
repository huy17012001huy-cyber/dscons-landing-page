## AGENTS ĐIỀU PHỐI

### Bước 1 — Curriculum Design
**Agent:** `education-training`
**Skills:** `course-curriculum-design`, `learning-objectives-writer`
- Learning objectives (Bloom's taxonomy)
- Module và lesson structure
- Assessment design
- Student workbook outline

### Bước 2 — Content Creation
**Agent:** `content-writer` + `education-training`
**Skills:** `lesson-script`, `workbook-design`
- Video lesson scripts
- Student workbook
- Supplementary materials
- Quiz questions

### Bước 3 — Sales Page
**Agent:** `content-writer` + `sales-funnel`
**Skills:** `sales-page-copy`, `customer-persona`
- Sales page copy
- Course description cho marketplace
- Testimonial framework
- FAQ section

### Bước 4 — Launch Email Sequence
**Agent:** `email-automation`
- Pre-launch waitlist sequence
- Launch week sequence (7 emails)
- Post-purchase onboarding
- Student success check-ins

### Bước 5 — Marketing
**Agent:** `social-media` + `content-writer`
- Pre-launch content strategy
- Social proof và testimonial collection
- Community building strategy

### Bước 6 — Ecommerce Setup
**Agent:** `ecommerce-product`
- Pricing strategy và tiers
- Order bump và upsell
- Affiliate program setup
- Payment và refund policy

## COURSE LAUNCH TIMELINE

```
T-8 tuần: Curriculum finalized
T-6 tuần: Beta cohort (validate) / Content creation begins
T-4 tuần: Sales page live, waitlist open
T-3 tuần: Pre-launch content campaign
T-2 tuần: Email warm-up sequence
T-1 tuần: Pre-launch events/webinar
Launch Week: 5-7 day open cart
T+2 tuần: Student onboarding complete
T+4 tuần: Collect first testimonials
```

---

## SUCCESS METRICS

```
Pre-launch: Waitlist size (target: 10x your enrollment goal)
Launch: Conversion rate (1-5% of list)
Post-launch:
- Completion rate (target >30%)
- Student NPS (>8/10)
- Testimonials collected
- Referral rate
```
