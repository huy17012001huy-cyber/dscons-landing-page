## AGENTS ĐIỀU PHỐI

### Bước 1 — Job Description
**Agent:** `hr-team`
**Skills:** `job-description`
- Viết JD hấp dẫn và chính xác
- Employer value proposition
- Requirements rõ ràng (must-have vs nice-to-have)

### Bước 2 — Hiring Process Design
**Agent:** `hr-team`
**Skills:** `hiring-process-design`, `interview-guide`
- Thiết kế quy trình tuyển (4-5 stages)
- Câu hỏi phỏng vấn theo competency
- Candidate scorecard

### Bước 3 — Legal Documents
**Agent:** `legal-compliance`
**Skills:** `employment-contract`, `offer-letter-template`, `nda-agreement`
- Offer letter template
- Employment contract
- NDA nếu cần

### Bước 4 — Onboarding Plan
**Agent:** `hr-team`
**Skills:** `onboarding-plan`, `new-hire-checklist`
- 30-60-90 day plan
- First week checklist
- Resources và tools setup

### Bước 5 — Training Plan
**Agent:** `hr-team` + `education-training`
**Skills:** `training-plan`
- Role-specific training
- Company knowledge transfer
- Tools và processes training

---

## HIRING PROCESS TEMPLATE

```
Stage 1: Application review (1-2 ngày)
Stage 2: Phone screen (20-30 phút)
Stage 3: Skills assessment / take-home task
Stage 4: Culture fit interview (45-60 phút)
Stage 5: Final interview + offer

Timeline target: 2-3 tuần từ đăng JD đến offer
```

---

## ONBOARDING CHECKLIST

```
Day 1:
□ Laptop + accounts setup
□ Team introduction
□ Culture và values overview
□ First week schedule

Week 1:
□ Meet all stakeholders
□ Read key documents/SOPs
□ First quick wins identified

Day 30: First formal check-in
Day 60: Performance review
Day 90: OKR setting + probation review
```
